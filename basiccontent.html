First semester

05.01.01 A Story Without Words

Our introduction into the theme of basic content was to direct, film and edit a short film, however we were tasked to tell the story without words. The group got together and settled on a idea for the film and with that direction in mind we went to work. During our lectures we learned about recording techniques, colour theory, equipment and about the basics of editing. Our group wanted to tell the story of burnout at work, so we created a set at a group members house that could depict an office/home. We focused on quick close ups of ordinary things to show the passing of time, such as waking up, making coffee and going to bed. We felt that this conveyed the passing of time. After filming was done we all sat down to work on editing, I had the most experience out our group, so I found it to be quite easy. We all worked on the editing until we were satisfied

05.02.02 Treatment

In this project we were tasked with creating three videos for a start-up company called Cherry pop and their focus was to improve sex education in Denmark. We were taught the concept of a treatment and how by following the path of theme, logline and finally the treatment itself we could streamline the starting process of making a film. The group sat down to brainstorm a few ideas and after our discussion we settled on an idea. With our idea in mind we went to work on our treatments, we all made one individually and then after discussing as a group decided on an treatment that followed the story of a kid struggling with access to safe and correct information regarding sex

05.03.01 Storyboard

Working in a group undermanned group we had to allocate jobs in a way so that we would be able to finish the project. In our lectures we were introduced to the importance of a Storyboard when it comes to film making. The Storyboard depicts the flow of the scenes throughout the film, such as camera angles, dialogue, audio and movement. However, I was tasked with filming the scenes and editing the films. So with a storyboard made by a group member I started filming. After gathering all my shots I sent them to the other members for approval. After getting approval from my group I started editing the films. In the editing process I was thankful for my experience in editing as it made the process easier, however I had to learn some new things to complete the project in they way we envisioned, such as how to create an coloured background with text and effects.

05.03.02 Customer Pitch

For the final project concerning the task set by Cherry pop and our teachers, we had to make a Peach Kucha presentation. After our lecture on Pecha Kucha where we were taught that these types of presentations are made in a way where each slide is short, concise and to the point. One of our members made the presentation and the rest had to write out a twenty second long text for each of our slides that we were to present.
