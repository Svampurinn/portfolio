First semester

03.01.01 Data Collection

During this module, I learned about how to conduct Market Research or in simpler terms, data collection. My objective was to find a common problem within the world of UX and bring forth a solution. So after learning about both desk and secondary research and how you can confirm your findings through interviews, surveys and questionnaires. So armed with this knowledge I conducted my research both online and by observing the problem in real time.

03.02.01 Early Prototype

For this assignment we were put into groups. Where I learned how to best work with other people when it comes to this kind of project. After being assigned a group I learned about different types of prototyping, such as the crazy 8, paper prototyping and UX storyboards. After sharing out initial sketches and prototypes the group came to a decision concerning the direction of the project. We decided on a web-shop that sells “designer eco-friendly t-shirts”. After working with the paper prototype and utilising the think aloud test we had a clearer goal to work towards.

03.03.01 Solution

Working in a group we allocated jobs to each member. I was tasked with Designing my own Illustrations that would be placed on a t-shirt for sale on the web-shop, I also wrote documented and wrote about our Content Decisions and finally I was supposed to design a logo for our “company” so I drew up some ideas and finalised them in Adobe Illustrator, however we ended up not using them for the final product. Through this whole process I learned about the intricacies that go into creating an illustration for a t-shirt, to be aware of how every decision needs to both cater to the user and to the design integrity. The importance of a persona was clear to me from the get go on how we should design our shop.
