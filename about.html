About Me

A 23 year old country bumpkin from Iceland making his way through all the different aspects of the digital world, born and raised in a small town outside of the capitol where all your needs are met, be it entertainment, education or love. I grew up in a household full of "the latest tech" because of my fathers occupation, with my hands full of devices I found love in all things digital. During my years in high school i came to the unfourtnate conclousion that i didn't have the brains for Computer Science and over time i fell in love with digital design. Armed with this revolation I set my sights on a higher education in Digital design, so I headed to Denmark. Today I'm a student at KEA enrolled in the Multimedia Design course.
